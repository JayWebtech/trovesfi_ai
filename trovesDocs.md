#### Q. Why did my holdings decrease in the xSTRK Sensei strategy?

If you noticed a decrease in your holdings before 9 February 2025, this is due to an upgrade that enhances the accuracy of the displayed balance. The new logic ensures your holdings reflect real values more precisely.

Furthermore, deposits made before this date might show a larger decrease due to a price difference between xSTRK's true exchange rate on Endur and its price on DEXs. This is normal market behaviour, as prices adjust based on the opportunity cost for liquidity providers.

**Will I ever recover from this loss?**&#x20;

Yes, there's a good chance you'll recover this in 4-6 weeks. As this is a leveraged position, the impact was slightly higher, but it should recover within this timeframe. This is based on the current APYs and market conditions.

**Why did this affect my holdings?**

In the past, when you deposited STRK, we used Endur to convert it into xSTRK. However, upon withdrawal, we had to sell xSTRK on DEXs to repay the leverage. This was the only way to obtain STRK instantly, but it meant your final holdings could be influenced by price differences between Endur and DEXs.

From 9 February 2025, we have enhanced the systemâ€”Endur is no longer utilised for deposits, and all transactions now occur through DEXs. While your holdings may still fluctuate based on xSTRKâ€™s price in DEXs (both positively and negatively), this approach is significantly better than before, as it eliminates the immediate price discrepancy that previously affected withdrawals.

**Why is there a price difference between xSTRK on Endur and DEXes?**

A 0.6â€“0.8% spread is normal and expected. The math behind this is:

Assuming the current STRK staking APY is 12%, a market maker on DEXs is supplying STRK, allowing you to instantly sell xSTRK for STRK. However, they could have used this STRK to stake instead and made a 12% APY. Since this represents an opportunity cost to them, they aim to generate enough from exchange fees and exchange rate spread to compensate for this opportunity cost. Therefore, if they accept your xSTRK and give you STRK, but then immediately unstake this xSTRK, they won't earn any yield for 21 days until they receive their STRK back. Consequently, the spread is approximately equivalent to the 21-day yield of Staking.

$$
\left(\frac{12\%}{52}\right) \times 3 = 0.7\%
$$

That said, the spread won't always remain this wide. It can fluctuate temporarily depending on buying and selling pressure in the market.

# Sensei - Delta neutral Lending

## What is Sensei - Delta Neutral Lending ?

There are 4 strategies available in delta neutral strategy:

* STRK Sensei&#x20;
* USDC Sensei&#x20;
* ETH Sensei
* xSTRK Sensei

Deposit your STRK/ USDC/ ETH to automatically loop your funds between zkLend and Nostra to create a delta neutral position. This strategy is designed to maximize your yield on USDC/ STRK/ ETH. Your position is automatically adjusted periodically to maintain a healthy health factor. You receive a NFT as representation for your stake on Troves. You can withdraw anytime by redeeming your NFT for USDC or STRK or ETH. For upto 2 weeks, your position value may reduce due to high borrow APR. This will be compensated by STRK rewards.

For xSTRK Sensei, we use Vesu and Re7's pools respectively.

## Why?

Using the Sensei strategy in Troves offers several benefits:

* **Expertly Curated Strategies**: Sensei Strats are designed by experienced DeFi strategists, ensuring optimal asset allocation and yield optimization.
* **Diversification**: These strategies often include a mix of assets and protocols, reducing risk through diversification.
* **Automatic Risk Management**: health of the contracts are monitored in real time and automatically rebalanced, allowing for a hands-off investment approach.
* **Enhanced Returns**: By leveraging advanced strategies and automated compounding, Sensei Strats aim to maximize your returns efficiently.

## Technical details:

#### Contract style - ERC721&#x20;



#### Technical flow of funds

Assuming you deposit $1000 worth of STRK to STRK sensei, initially your tokens will be deposited to zkLend supply. Based on the $1000 deposit in ZkLend, a borrow will be taken equivalent to $384.21 of ETH which is calculated such that we maintain a target health factor (e.g. 1.3) overall from ZkLend which will be deposited to Nostra Money Markets' ETH supply. Furthermore, approximately $189.149 of STRK is borrowed from Nostra Money Markets corresponding to the $381.21 of ETH supplied which is again calculated such that we maintain a target health factor. The same process of depositing STRK starts from the beginning by depositing Nostra's borrow to ZkLend supply and the whole process is repeated 3 more times.

In conclusion, 4 steps of borrows of supply and borrow are taken which compounds the overall interest of user's deposit to a higher TVL. Furthermore, every 2 weeks, the strategy deposits your STRK rewards to ZkLend which appreciates your interest even more.

# Vesu Fusion - Rebalancing vaults

[Vesu](https://vesu.xyz/) is a fully open and permissionless lending protocol built on Starknet. Users can lend crypto assets to earn, borrow assets, and build new lending experiences on Vesu without intermediaries. Vesu is built as a public infrastructure free for everyone to use.

### What are rebalancing vaults?

Vesu lets you create different [pools](https://vesu.xyz/pools) with their own curated assets. These can be used as collateral and borrowed. Independent curators can make custom pools with different risk levels. For example, Vesu has already seen curators like [Re7](https://re7.capital/) and [Alterscope](https://www.alterscope.org/) launch their pools. The downside of this approach is liquidity fragmentation. As a liquidity supplier, you may need to constantly monitor and rebalance your funds across multiple pools to optimize your yield for best returns. Thatâ€™s where we come inâ€”automating this process while ensuring diversification.

Second, it's cool.

#### Terminology:

### Risk framework:

1. We only **use verified pools** on Vesu to invest funds (i.e. Genesis, Re7 and Alterscope pools)
2. We will only stick to low and medium risk pools (for now).
3. Each pool has a maximum weight parameter configured, which determines the exposure the vault can take. The weight configuration is determined by the following factors:
   1. **The quality of collateral tokens in the pool:** If a pool is fully made of blue-chip collateral tokens, the maximum weight of the pool will be higher compared to others.&#x20;
   2. **The history of the pool:** Pools with less than 3-month history will be given less weightage over established pools. This is just an added cool-down period to avoid taking higher risk on new pools. However, there can be exceptions depending on the reputation of pool curator.&#x20;

### Understanding Position States in Concentrated Liquidity

When you deposit into the CL vault, your funds are used to provide liquidity within a specific price range on Ekubo (similar to Uniswap V3). Depending on where the current market price is relative to that range, your position might be held in xSTRK, STRK, or a mix of both.

Hereâ€™s how vault behaves:

* **Price is below the range** â†’ Your position is completely in **xSTRK**
